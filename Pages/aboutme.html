Introduction

Personal Insights and Values

Lifelong Learning

I strongly value continuous learning and personal growth. Whether through formal education, certifications, or independent study, I believe staying curious and open to new knowledge is essential for both personal and professional development.

Integrity and Accountability

I believe in doing the right thing—even when no one is watching. Integrity and accountability are values I hold deeply, and I strive to reflect them in every project, relationship, and decision I make.

Discipline and Focus

Discipline has always been a core part of my success, whether it’s mastering a piece on the violin or working through a complex business analysis. I value staying focused, setting goals, and following through with consistency and care.

Curiosity and Problem-Solving

I’m naturally curious and love tackling challenges. I enjoy digging into problems, asking questions, and finding smart, practical solutions—especially in areas like business strategy and analytics.

Collaboration and Respect

I believe the best ideas come from collaboration and respectful dialogue. I value listening to others, sharing knowledge, and building strong relationships that lead to meaningful results.

Excellence and Growth Mindset

I aim for excellence in everything I do but also understand the importance of learning from failure. A growth mindset helps me stay resilient and constantly improve.

Hobbies

Puzzles & Problem-Solving

I enjoy logic puzzles, brainteasers, and strategy games that challenge my thinking and improve my ability to approach problems from different angles. This hobby complements my interest in analytics and strategic planning.

Playing the Violin

Music has been a meaningful part of my life for years. Playing the violin has taught me discipline, focus, and the value of consistent practice—skills that carry over into both academic and professional environments.

Learning & Self-Education

I have a genuine love for learning. Whether it's through formal courses, certifications, or independent study, I enjoy exploring new topics—especially those related to business, finance, and technology.

Listening to Music

I enjoy listening to music throughout the day, especially jazz, big band, bossa nova, and classical. Music helps me focus, stay creative, and unwind—it’s a constant source of energy and inspiration.

Reading

Personal Interests

My personal interests are driven by a deep passion for learning and a relentless work ethic.
